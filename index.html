Creamy Crab And Corn Soup

crab and ginger area winning combination. Herewith the addtion of creamd corn, they make a most delistion velvety-textured soup. rich and satisfying it is vrey similar to a chowder

  • 1small cooked crab
  • 2egg whits
  • 1tb corn starch mixed fresh ginger root
  • 1tb corn strach mixed with 2-3 tbs milk
  • cups chinese stock (page 12)
  • 8-0z can creamed corn (see box, right)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Thinly sliced scallion rings for garnish
  1. Remove both the white and dark crabmeat from from the shell flaking it into a bowl wit a skewer.Beat the egg whits in another bowl until just frothy.Peel and finely chop the ginger
  2. Add the chopped ginger to the flaked crabmeat and stir well to mix, then add the egg whites and the cornstrach paste and blend well to mix all the ingreddients toghter
  3. Bring the stock to a rolling boli in a wok,add the the corn and bring buck to a boil. Add the crabmeat mictrue and salt and pepper to teste.Serve at once sprrinkled with scallion rings.
